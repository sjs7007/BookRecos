The Fellowship of the Ring
The first volume in J.R.R. Tolkien's epic adventure <em>The Lord of the Rings</em>. In a sleepy village in the Shire a young hobbit is entrusted with a immense task. He must make a perilous journey across Middle-earth to the Cracks of Doom, there to destroy the Ruling Ring of Power - the only thing that prevents the Dark Lord's evil dominion.
